ABOUT THE LAW OFFICE

PASLARU LAW OFFICE was set up in December 2011 by Ms. Nicoleta Paslaru, a member of the New York (2008) and Bucharest Bar (2009), who, until then, had been working both with one of the major law firms in Romania and with a boutique law firm.

PASLARU LAW OFFICE was created out of the desire to offer clients the reliable traditional legal values while providing them an opportunity for future growth.

With the common law knowledge accumulated during her LLB in Law and Politics at Hull University, United Kingdom (2000-2003), her LLM in European Law at King’s College London, United Kingdom (2000-2004) and preparation for the New York Bar Examination (registered since 2008), next, from 2009, Ms. Paslaru has included the grasp of Romanian law among her expertise.

At PASLARU LAW OFFICE, the RESPECT, PROTECTION and DEVELOPMENT of the Client are the core principles that guide its activity.
